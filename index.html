Do you want to know how to make Red Velvet Cupcakes?


If you do this website is for you!


The ingredients you need will be:
1 and 1/3 cups cake flour (spooned & leveled)
2 tablespoons natural unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup unsalted butter softened to room temperature
1 cup granulated sugar
1 large egg room temperature
1/4 cup oil canola or vegetable work well
1 tablespoon liquid red or pink food coloring
1 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
2/3 cup buttermilk room temperature
CREAM CHEESE FROSTING:
1/2 cup unsalted butter softened to room temperature
1 (8-ounce) package cream cheese softened to room temperature
2 cups powdered sugar
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS:
1) Preheat oven to 350째F (177째C).
2) Line two standard 12-count muffin pans with 16 cupcake liners and set aside.
3) In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the cake flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Then sift the dry ingredients to remove any lumps of cocoa powder. Set it aside.
4) In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, or in a large mixing bowl using an electric mixer, mix together the butter and sugar on medium speed for 4-5 minutes.
5) Add the egg and mix until fully combined. Then mix in the oil, red food coloring, and vanilla.
6) Mix in the dry ingredients in three additions alternating with the buttermilk, starting and ending with the dry ingredients.
7) Make sure to mix each addition until just combined and be careful not to over mix the batter.
8) Evenly distribute the batter between all 16 cupcake liners in the prepared muffin pans, only filling each one about halfway full.
9) Bake in separate batches at 350째F (177째C) for 15-18 minutes or until the tops of the cupcakes spring back when lightly touched and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
10) Carefully remove the cupcakes from the pan and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

TO MAKE CREAM CHEESE FROSTING:
1) In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, or in a large mixing bowl using an electric mixer, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
2) Add the butter and mix for about 30 seconds-1 minute until well combined and smooth.
3) Add in the powdered sugar and vanilla extract and continue mixing until fully combined, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed.
4) Once the cupcakes have cooled completely, pipe the frosting on top.
5) Frost the cupcakes using a large zip top bag with the corner snipped off put the frosting tip about a half inch above the cupcake, squeeze frosting onto the cupcake and go in circles then pull up.
